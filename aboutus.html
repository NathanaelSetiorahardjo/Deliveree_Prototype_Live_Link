ABOUT DELIVEREE

Connecting businesses and individuals with fast, reliable, and efficient delivery solutions across Indonesia

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Deliveree was born from a simple vision: to revolutionize the logistics industry in Indonesia by providing accessible, efficient, and technology-driven delivery solutions.


What started as a small team of logistics enthusiasts has now grown into Indonesia's leading delivery service provider, with operations spanning across major cities and a growing network of professional drivers committed to excellence.


Our journey has been driven by our relentless pursuit of innovation, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence. Today, we handle over 25,000 deliveries daily, serving businesses of all sizes and individuals who trust us with their packages.

Our Mission

To transform Indonesia's logistics landscape by leveraging technology and exceptional service, delivering not just packages but trust, reliability, and peace of mind to our customers.
